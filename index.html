Compa App

Travel application that consolidates all your travel needs.

Research

5 interviews were scheduled over FaceTime Audio to answer the following research objective, “What are the most important factors when planning travel?” through the research questions.

Organized user research data for the 5 interviewees. Created 4 categories for the user research data. Removed redundant ideas from the user research data to provide clear and concise Affinity Diagram.

Problem Statement

Our Compa app was designed to achieve a stress free way of managing your budget while on travel. We have observed that our product or service isn’t meeting helping travellers manage their budget effectively, which is causing this to reflect negatively to our business travel partners. How might we improve the Compa app so that our customers are successful based on staying within the budget goal?
